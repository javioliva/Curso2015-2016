- Use of slash URIs

The domain will be www.worldstuff.org and the URI defined for a resource will follow this format: www.worldstuff.org/type/individual#fragment

type will be replaced by the name of the type of the element we are accessing, for example "country" or "city".

individual will be replaced by the defining entity of the individual we want to access,

and #fragment will be included just in case we want to refer to just a part of the individual.
