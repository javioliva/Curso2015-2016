Estrategia de nombrado

Hash URIs for ontological terms, slash URIs for individuals:

- Domain: http://example.linkeddata.es/

- Ontological terms path: http://example.linkeddata.es/my_vocabulary#

- Individuals path: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/

- Ontological terms pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/my_vocabulary#

- Individuals pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances//
